Paul Glaser is a designer

Jennette McCurdy dated the author of her own shoe ‘iCarly’ for a limited period of time. McCurdy was 18 when the final season of iCarly aired, and Paul Glaser was 13 years her senior. Because of the large age difference, some people on social media accused Paul Glaser of grooming her into being with him. McCurdy, on the other hand, holds a different viewpoint. She told Vulture in an interview:

“People would make very rude comments about him online, saying things like, ‘What a creep to have had an eye on her for so long.'” But they didn’t know the complete story, and that’s when I truly despised social media, thinking, ‘These people are making it hard for me.'”

During the same interview, she stated:

“I directed my feelings of love away from my mother, who had been the recipient of all that love up until that point, because I knew she was leaving, and I was panicking, and I think there was just a part of myself that was ready to redirect these feelings of love toward a new person.”

When her mother’s condition worsened, she said:

“I needed to go through that loss on my own because I was afraid of putting too much of myself into another person and becoming overly attached.” ‘Oh, if I share this grief with someone, and this part of myself with someone, and this reality with someone, then they become my reality,’ I reasoned. That concept terrified me.”
