Taylor Swift's bodyguard makes a return to Israel to combat Hamas war after gaining fame for protect

March 2024 · 1 minute read

Having returned to his homeland in Israel, Taylor Swift’s bodyguard has volunteered for the Israeli Defence Forces (IDF) to participate in the fight against the Hamas war.

The security guard garnered significant attention during Taylor Swift’s “Eras Tour” in the past summer due to his remarkable dedication in protecting the singer.

The unnamed guard, reportedly a former member of the Israel Defense Forces (IDF) prior to his time in America, is said to have returned to Israel as he refused to stand by while families were being slaughtered and burned alive in their homes.

The bodyguard recently posted an image of himself in his military uniform on his Facebook page, accompanied by a statement that expressed, “I Stand with Israel… but it shouldn’t be limited to one individual supporting the sole Jewish state. It should signify ‘I stand with humanity!'”.

“While one side is protecting babies kids and the elderly, the other side is using those as human shields,” he added.
